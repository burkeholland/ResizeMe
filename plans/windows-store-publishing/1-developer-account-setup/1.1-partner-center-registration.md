# Step 1.1: Microsoft Partner Center Developer Account Registration

**Part of:** Step 1 from plan.md  
**Focus:** Register and verify Microsoft Partner Center developer account for Windows Store publishing  
**File to Create:** Developer account setup documentation  
**Estimated Time:** 30-60 minutes (plus verification time)

## Overview

This substep creates a Microsoft Partner Center developer account, which is required to publish apps to the Microsoft Store. The account provides access to submission, analytics, and store management tools.

## Pre-Substep Checklist

- [ ] **Branch:** Working on `feature/windows-store-publishing` (from plan.md)
- [ ] **Current Step:** Starting Step 1 - Developer Account Setup
- [ ] **Requirements:** Valid Microsoft account, payment method for registration fee, business information

## Implementation

### Goal
Register and verify a Microsoft Partner Center developer account to enable Windows Store app submission.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

#### Step 1: Navigate to Partner Center Registration
- [ ] Open web browser and go to https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/registration/developer
- [ ] Sign in with your Microsoft account (create one if needed)
- [ ] Select account type:
  - **Individual**: $19 USD registration fee, simpler verification
  - **Company**: $99 USD registration fee, requires business verification

#### Step 2: Complete Registration Form
- [ ] Fill out required information:
  - Contact details (name, address, phone)
  - Tax information (for payouts)
  - Payment method for registration fee
- [ ] For company accounts, provide:
  - Business name and address
  - Business verification documents
  - D-U-N-S number (if applicable)

#### Step 3: Pay Registration Fee and Submit
- [ ] Review registration details carefully
- [ ] Pay the registration fee ($19 individual / $99 company)
- [ ] Submit registration for review
- [ ] Note: Processing time is typically 24-72 hours

#### Step 4: Complete Identity Verification
- [ ] Check email for verification requests from Microsoft
- [ ] Complete any additional verification steps:
  - Email verification (immediate)
  - Phone verification (if required)
  - Business verification documents (company accounts)
- [ ] Wait for approval notification

#### Step 5: Access Partner Center Dashboard
- [ ] Once approved, sign in to https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard
- [ ] Verify access to "Apps and games" section
- [ ] Note your Publisher ID and Publisher Display Name for later use

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Registration fee paid and processed
- [ ] Email confirmation received from Microsoft Partner Center
- [ ] Can access Partner Center dashboard at https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard
- [ ] "Apps and games" section is visible and accessible
- [ ] Publisher information is correctly displayed

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Registration fee payment fails | Try different payment method; ensure billing address matches card |
| Identity verification delayed | Check spam folder for emails; contact Partner Center support if >72 hours |
| Business verification required | Provide D-U-N-S number and official business documents |
| Can't access dashboard after approval | Clear browser cache; try incognito mode; check Microsoft account permissions |
